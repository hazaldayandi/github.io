Privacy Policy for HoraryNow

Effective date: February 27, 2026

HoraryNow (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we process when you use the HoraryNow mobile app.

1. Data We Process

a) Question and Reading Data

  • Question text
  • Reading context you provide (location, date/time, timezone, coordinates if available)
  • Generated AI response

If cloud history is enabled, we may store:

  • Question text
  • Short reading summary/preview
  • Timestamp
  • Anonymous app user identifier

History records are retained for up to 30 days.

b) Device and App Data

  • App version
  • Device type / OS version
  • Basic diagnostics and error logs

c) Advertising Data

HoraryNow uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect or receive data such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate location, and interaction/performance data.

Google policies:
https://policies.google.com/privacy
https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide horary reading functionality
  • Return responses in the appropriate language
  • Save and display history (if enabled)
  • Maintain app security and performance
  • Serve and measure ads

3. Third-Party Services

HoraryNow may use third-party services, including:

  • Google AdMob (ads)
  • Supabase (history storage / backend data)
  • AI model/API providers (reading generation)

Each provider processes data under its own privacy terms.

4. Permissions

Where required, we request permission before accessing certain data (for example, location). You can deny permissions and still use core app features with manually entered context.

5. Data Retention

  • Local app data may remain on your device until deleted.
  • Cloud history (if enabled) is retained for up to 30 days unless deleted earlier.
  • Some diagnostic/log data may be retained for operational and security needs.

6. Your Choices

  • Avoid sharing optional information
  • Delete app history from within the app (where available)
  • Uninstall the app to remove local data

For ad personalization controls, use your device privacy/ad settings.

7. Children

HoraryNow is not directed to children under the age defined by applicable law in your region.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data in transit and at rest. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own, depending on our service providers’ infrastructure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new effective date.
